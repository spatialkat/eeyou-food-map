📋 About this map

This map compiles food security initiatives across Eeyou Istchee. It brings together programs, projects, and organizations that support access to food, food production and knowledge related to traditional foods, nutritious store-bought foods, and community-produced foods.

🎯 Goal

To provide a comprehensive, centralized reference of food-related initiatives across Eeyou Istchee. To support coordination, planning, and decision-making for communities, organizations, and partners.

This map was produced by the Cree Nation Government Climate Change Unit to better understand climate change impacts on food security. By mapping existing initiatives, it helps identify strengths and gaps, and can inform climate adaptation strategies.

👥 Who should use this map

Community members, band council staff, researchers, government partners, and anyone interested in understanding the landscape of food security efforts in Eeyou Istchee.

While it supports climate change adaptation planning, it can also be used more broadly for program development, collaboration, and knowledge sharing.

🗺️ How to use

Use the sidebar filters on the left to narrow initiatives by community, organization, food source, or initiative type. Use the search bar to find specific initiatives by name. Click any marker on the map or any entry in the sidebar list to see full details. The list updates automatically as you pan or zoom the map.

✏️ How to contribute

Know of an initiative that's missing, or noticed something that needs updating? Use the contribution form to submit a new initiative or suggest a correction. You can also access the form at any time using the ✏️ button below the info button on the map.

Food Production & HarvestingIncreases supplies of food (ex: Greenhouses, community hunts, harvesting initiatives)
